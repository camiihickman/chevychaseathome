CHEVY CHASE AT HOME is a non-profit, volunteer-driven organization for seniors in the Chevy Chase area. The mission of Chevy Chase At Home is to keep us connected and active in our community with neighbors of all ages as we age in place.

Coronavirus Update

Unfortunately, the coronavirus is constricting many of our daily behaviors and activities.
In an abundance of caution, CC@H has cancelled scheduled special events in March.
Leadership is actively monitoring the Center for Disease Control (CDC) and Maryland state plus Montgomery County recommendations and is in constant communications with similar local nonprofits to develop consistent and thoughtful procedures.

Although folks should avoid large group gatherings and convening indoors, you can still have fun, socialize, and get exercise.
Meet outdoors to walk, talk, and enjoy spring blossoms!

Exercise Classes

Drop-in options are available -- $20 for any class.
Call the office before the class.
Our small classes are held in the spacious Chevy Chase Village Hall.
The facility now has extra sanitation procedures and classes are held with windows open for fresh air and maximum ventilation.
We ask folks to refrain from attending if they are feeling unwell or have reason to self isolate.

Inclement Weather Policy

 Chevy Chase At Home follows the Montgomery County
Public Schools (MCPS) inclement weather policy.
When MCPS open late, or are closed,
CC@H opens late or is closed accordingly.
